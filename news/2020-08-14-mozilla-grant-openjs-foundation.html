Mozilla MOSS grant and OpenJS Foundation

August 14, 2020

(opens new window) (opens new window)

Ajv has been awarded a grant from Mozilla’s Open Source Support (MOSS) program (opens new window) in the “Foundational Technology” track! It will sponsor the development of Ajv support of JSON Schema version 2019-09 (opens new window) and of JSON Type Definition (RFC8927) (opens new window).

Ajv also joined OpenJS Foundation (opens new window) – having this support will help ensure the longevity and stability of Ajv for all its users.

This blog post (opens new window) has more details.

I am looking for the long term maintainers of Ajv – working with ReadySet (opens new window), also sponsored by Mozilla, to establish clear guidelines for the role of a "maintainer" and the contribution standards, and to encourage a wider, more inclusive, contribution from the community.
